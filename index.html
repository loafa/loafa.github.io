Ice Renderer

6.837 (Computer Graphics) Final Project

Implementation of a ray tracer for realistic and "Disney-fied" ice renderings by simulating reflection, refraction, sub-surface scattering, and caustics.

Awarded an Honorable Mention in the 6.837 Fall 2016 Class.

Project Express

MIT Media Lab Research Project [Fluid Interfaces]

A system to facilitate visual art techniques as a form of expression, "Project Express" is a tool to provide people with muscular disabilities access to painting. With it, any data-transmitting device can be used as a painting tool.

MoveMe

MIT Media Lab Research Project [Fluid Interfaces]

MoveMe is a haptic music education system that facilitates teaching over long distances and provides superior tactile feedback during individual practice sessions.

Awarded the 2015 ACE Bronze Prize.

TInk is one of the Texas Instruments summer internship projects. Interns use TI microprocessors and development software to add functionality to a toy tank and are responsible for reporting any bugs they come across.
